Need to get rid of medicine but can’t donate?

If any of your medications are not eligible for donation, please dispose of them properly by dropping them off at a local collection site. If there is not a convenient collection site and the medications are not controlled substances, you can also dispose of them yourself: simply cross off your information from the prescription bottle, mix the medicine with something unappetizing (such as coffee grounds or kitty litter), and place it into your household trash. Learn more about proper disposal from the FDA.
